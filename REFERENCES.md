## Audio Transport â€” Prior Art

| Platform                                                                                                                                | What Inspired OpenPod                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Signal** ([RingRTC](https://github.com/nickclaw/nickclaw))                                                                            | RingRTC (68% Rust) proves Rust is production-viable for real-time audio SFU (40+ participants). | **Telegram** ([MTProto](https://core.telegram.org/mtproto))                                                                             | Emoji-based call verification is analogous to OpenPod's 6-digit SAS. | **Mumble** ([protocol docs](https://mumble-protocol.readthedocs.io/))                                                                   | Closest architectural analog: custom binary framing (not RTP), 1-byte type prefix, Opus codec, UDP+TCP split. Mumble's TCP fallback for degraded networks informed the "promote to stream" fallback consideration.                                              |
